Project Shree Ram is an independent Hindu–Jewish cultural and advocacy nonprofit. We are not a government body, a staffing agency, or a placement company; we process no job applications and charge no fees.

All labor applications, selection, contracts, and travel between India and Israel are handled entirely by the two governments and their authorized agencies. Beware of anyone charging fees or promising jobs — official government portals never do.
