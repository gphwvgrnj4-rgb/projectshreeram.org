Who We Are

A cultural bridge, not a recruiter

Project Shree Ram is an independent, nonprofit cultural and advocacy initiative. We are not a government body, a staffing agency, or a placement company. We do not process job applications, charge fees, or place workers. Our purpose is to celebrate the historic friendship between Hindus and Jews and to help Indian citizens find the correct, official channels already established between the Governments of India and Israel.

A Torah Concept

Ger Toshav -- the righteous resident

In Torah tradition, a Ger Toshav is a non-Jewish resident who lives among the Jewish people, upholds the universal Noahide principles, and in turn is owed protection, dignity, and a place of honor in the land. It is a framework of mutual respect and covenant, not conquest -- one people welcoming another who shares its ethical foundations. We believe the historic bond between Hindus and Jews, two of the world's oldest continuous civilizations, echoes that same spirit of hospitality and shared moral seriousness.
