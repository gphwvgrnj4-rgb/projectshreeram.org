Privacy Policy

How we handle the limited information you choose to share with us.

This page is a working draft, pending review by a qualified lawyer in the relevant jurisdictions. It is provided in good faith for transparency and is not yet legal advice.

Project Shree Ram (“we”) is a nonprofit cultural and advocacy initiative. This policy explains how we handle the limited information you choose to share with us.

What we collect

Only what you enter into our forms — typically your name, email, city, an optional messaging number, and any message or comment. We do not run advertising trackers or sell data.

How we use it

To reply to you, to keep you updated on our cultural and advocacy work if you ask us to, and to count petition signatures. Form submissions are delivered to our team through our forms provider (Formspree).

What we never do

We never charge fees, never pass your details to any government agency or recruiter, and never sell your information.

Your choices

You can ask us to delete your information at any time by contacting us through the Contact page.

Project Shree Ram is an independent Hindu–Jewish cultural and advocacy nonprofit. We are not a government body, a staffing agency, or a placement company; we process no job applications and charge no fees.

All labor applications, selection, contracts, and travel between India and Israel are handled entirely by the two governments and their authorized agencies. Beware of anyone charging fees or promising jobs — official government portals never do.
