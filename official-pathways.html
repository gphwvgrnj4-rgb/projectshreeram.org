Official India–Israel Work Pathways

The correct, fee-free, government-run channels — and how to spot and avoid recruitment scams.

The Framework

How Indians can work in Israel, legally

India and Israel have an active government-to-government (G2G) labor mobility framework covering sectors such as elder care, agriculture, and construction. These programs are run entirely by the two governments — not by Project Shree Ram. If you are interested in working in Israel, the correct and only legitimate first step is through the official portals below.

Where to Apply

Official government channels

Verified official portals

  • Uttar Pradesh Rojgar Sangam — Israel Vacancy Portal
  • Skills2Jobs — Israel Caregiver & Worker Opportunities
Applications, selection, contracts, and travel are handled entirely by the Indian and Israeli governments and their authorized agencies. Project Shree Ram has no role in this process and cannot influence outcomes, speed up review, or guarantee placement.

Stay Safe

How to avoid job scams

This is a space targeted by fraudsters. A few rules protect you:

  • Official government portals never charge a fee to apply.
  • No genuine process offers a “guaranteed job” or “guaranteed visa” for a payment.
  • Never pay an agent for a place in a government quota.
  • Check any offer, message, or agent against the official portals above before acting.
  • Never share passport scans, bank details, or payments with an unverified “recruiter.”
If someone asks you for money to secure an Israel job, it is a scam. Walk away and report it.
